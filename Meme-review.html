This meme-review is all about Bongo Cat!

The tale of a short lived meme

The bongo cat meme is all about musical cats, it is a meme with endless possibilities. The meme unfortunately had a quick death because of the rise of the moth memes, which still mindblows me every single day. The moth meme is in my opinion less of a meme in comparison to Bongo Cat, mostly because the endless creative possibilities of bongo cat, just weren't there with the moth memes.

As you might have noticed my colleague Pewdiepie is very fond of the Bongo Cat meme as well. I must also recommend you, of course, watch the full video.

Short lived maybe, but was it any good while it was still alive?

In my opinion, yes. The meme combinations that were made and never knowing what the end would lead to made the bong cat one of the best memes of september. Unfortunately it got overshadowed by the pure stupidity of the moth memes. In a way moth memes weren't supposed to be able to overtake bongo cat in popularity, not even by a long shot. Bongo cat is the perfect meme on paper, while the moth meme isn't at all. The weird thing is that I can't name what is bad about the moth meme it's just bad. I guess that we're missing a factor in our way of predicting rises in meme popularity, the mystery meme factor, the thing that makes memes populair while nobody understands the joke. I think that is what bongo cat was missing, but what the moth meme had.

Conclusion time

Bongo cat gets a 4 from Pewd's, but personally I'd give Bongo cat a 7. So the average is a 5,5 which is barely a pass, but good enough. *Claps twice* Meme review.
